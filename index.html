About Suitable English Course (SEC)

SEC is a series of English courses for junior high school, senior high school, and university students. The materials developed in this school have adapted the teaching-learning method which uses genres as the main tool in language learning. They have been carefully designed to meet students' expectations in learning a foreign language ‚Äî to express their feelings and ideas.

All activities in SEC cover the four language skills: listening, speaking, reading, and writing. They encourage students to develop their competence in academic English.
